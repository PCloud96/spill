Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 6, 2026

Cloud Motion Lab LLC ("we," "our," or "us") built Spill: Truth or Dare ("the App") as a commercial application. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information when you use our App.

Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. The App does not require an account, login, or registration. Player names entered during gameplay are stored only on your device and are never sent to any server.

Camera and Microphone

The App does not access your camera, microphone, photos, contacts, or location.

Advertising

The App displays contextual ads provided by Unity Ads. These ads do not use personal data or device tracking for targeting. We do not use Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework because no user tracking occurs. Unity Ads may collect non-personal technical data (such as device model and OS version) to serve ads. For more information, see Unity's Privacy Policy.

In-App Purchases

The App offers in-app purchases processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not collect or have access to your payment information. All purchase management, including refunds and cancellations, is handled through your Apple ID account settings.

Data Storage

All app data, including player names, game preferences, and purchase status, is stored locally on your device. No data is transmitted to external servers.

Children's Privacy

The App includes content modes intended for users aged 18 and older, which are gated behind an age verification screen. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe your child has provided personal information, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

• Unity Ads — for displaying contextual advertisements
• Apple StoreKit — for processing in-app purchases

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
